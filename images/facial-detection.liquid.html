Use the bounding box tool to draw boxes around the faces

Detailed Instructions

  1. Begin by carefully examining the entire image.
  2. When you indentify a human face, draw a rectangle using your mouse over it
  3. Make sure the box does not cut into the face and leave a 2 - 3 pixel margin around the face
  4. When there are overlapping faces, draw a box around each face, include all contiguous parts of the target in the box. Do not include parts that are completely overlapped by another object.
  5. Do not include parts of the target that cannot be seen, even though you think you can interpolate the whole shape of the face
  6. If the face goes partially off screen, still label up to the edge of the image
